What Is Hijama Therapy?

Hijama, derived from the Arabic word meaning "to restore to basic size," is a form of cupping therapy with roots in Islamic prophetic medicine and ancient healing traditions. It involves placing specially designed cups on the skin to create a vacuum that draws blood to the surface, improving local circulation and stimulating the lymphatic system.

Both dry cupping (suction only) and wet cupping (Hijama with superficial skin incisions) are offered. The therapy is believed to remove stagnant blood and toxins, reduce inflammation, ease chronic pain, and boost overall wellbeing. When integrated with physiotherapy, it can enhance recovery outcomes for musculoskeletal and pain conditions.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Individuals with chronic back, neck, or shoulder pain
  • Those seeking natural, non-pharmaceutical pain relief
  • Athletes with muscle tension, fatigue, or sports injuries
  • Patients with migraine, hypertension, or metabolic concerns
  • Those looking for holistic wellness and immune support alongside physiotherapy
