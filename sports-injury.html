What Is Sports Physiotherapy?

Sports physiotherapy is a specialised branch of physiotherapy focused on preventing and treating injuries sustained during sporting activities or exercise. It covers acute injuries — such as muscle tears, ligament sprains, and fractures — as well as chronic overuse conditions like tendinopathies, stress fractures, and repetitive strain injuries.

Beyond treating the injury, sports physiotherapy addresses the biomechanical and conditioning factors that caused it. Our goal is not just recovery but optimised physical performance — ensuring you return to sport stronger, more resilient, and less likely to be injured again.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Athletes of all levels with acute muscle, tendon, or joint injuries
  • Runners with shin splints, IT band syndrome, or plantar fasciitis
  • Cricket, football, and badminton players with overuse or impact injuries
  • Gym-goers with training-related pain or postural imbalances
  • Anyone wanting injury prevention screening and performance optimisation
